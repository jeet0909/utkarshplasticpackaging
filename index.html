About Us

Established in 1995 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Utkarsh Plastic Packaging has emerged as a premier national leader in high-performance packaging and polymer solutions. Under the strategic leadership of Mr. Nikunj Patel, we operate a sophisticated manufacturing facility with a capacity to process 100 to 150 metric tons of raw material monthly.

With three decades of industrial excellence, we have earned the unwavering trust of over 200 valued clients across India. Driven by a dedicated workforce of 35 skilled professionals, our plant maintains a high-velocity production rate of 40,000 bags per day.

Our integrated manufacturing approach combines advanced technology with deep expertise to serve critical sectors such as agriculture, chemicals, and food processing. From UV-stabilized woven sacks to premium PP reprocessed granules, our portfolio is designed to set industry benchmarks through reliable supply chains and sustainable practices.
